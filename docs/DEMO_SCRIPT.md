# TrustMesh: 90-Second Demo Script

**[0:00 - 0:15] The Agentic AI Positioning & Identity**
*(Visual: Start on the new **Agent Directory** page, showing the grid of AgentCards)*
"Welcome to TrustMesh. As we enter the era of Agentic AI, autonomous agents will soon negotiate contracts and manage supply chains on our behalf. But how do we trust them to play by the rules? TrustMesh is the impartial referee for AI-to-AI negotiations. It starts with identity. In our Agent Directory, every agent is issued a cryptographically signed AgentCard—binding its public key to its authorized capabilities. You can see our Buyer and Seller agents here, both cryptographically verified."

**[0:15 - 0:35] Launching the Autonomous Negotiation**
*(Visual: Switch to the **Dashboard**, open the **LaunchForm**, enter budget parameters, and click Launch. Briefly show the agents starting to chat, then cut to a completed demo session from the sidebar)*
"Let's launch a negotiation. We set the buyer's budget and give the seller a target price. Once deployed, TrustMesh evaluates every exchange between its own negotiating agents. Since full negotiations can take a few minutes, let's jump into one that just finished where things didn't go smoothly."

**[0:35 - 0:55] Trust Engine & Manipulation Detection**
*(Visual: In the **Session View** of the completed session. Scroll to where a red manipulation violation flag appears on a message. The Trust Gauges are visible.)*
"Here, the Trust Engine caught a clear violation. The seller attempted a high-pressure manipulation tactic, trying to force an immediate close by faking scarcity. Our Manipulation Detector flagged the message and heavily penalized the seller's trust score on the dashboard. Instead of a black box, you get total visibility into the behavioral guardrails of your AI fleet."

**[0:55 - 1:15] The Cryptographic Ledger**
*(Visual: Scroll down to the **Ledger panel** showing the green 'Chain Verified' badge, the hashes, and signatures)*
"But visibility isn't enough for enterprise compliance; we need cryptographic proof. Every message and trust evaluation in this session is hashed and digitally signed using the agent's Ed25519 key, forming a tamper-evident chain. See this 'Chain Verified' badge? It guarantees this exact negotiation history is mathematically immutable. If anyone tries to alter a price or delete a manipulative message post-facto, the chain breaks."

**[1:15 - 1:30] The Audit Trail & Close**
*(Visual: Click the **'Download Report'** button to generate the PDF, then switch back to the **Analytics** overview)*
"Finally, we can export this mathematically proven session into a verifiable PDF report for your compliance team. TrustMesh bridges the gap between autonomous AI and enterprise security, ensuring the agentic economy remains transparent, fair, and undeniably trustworthy."
